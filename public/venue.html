International Centre for Interdisciplinary Science and Education (ICISE), Quy Nhon, Vietnam

ICISE Quy Nhon

The International Centre for Interdisciplinary Science and Education (ICISE) is a world-class venue for scientific conferences and education.

Located in the beautiful coastal city of Quy Nhon, Central Vietnam, ICISE provides an inspiring environment for scientific collaboration and discovery. The center was established with the ambitious objective to focus on developing Science and Education by fostering exchanges between Asia-Pacific scientists and colleagues from other parts of the world.

About Quy Nhon

Quy Nhon is a coastal city in Binh Dinh province, Central Vietnam, known for its pristine beaches, rich history, and vibrant culture. The city offers a perfect blend of ancient traditions and modern development.

Visitors can explore beautiful beaches, historic Cham towers, and enjoy fresh seafood. The city's strategic location makes it easily accessible while providing a peaceful environment ideal for scientific conferences.

The climate in Quy Nhon is tropical, with warm temperatures year-round. September-October offers pleasant weather with comfortable temperatures and lower humidity, making it perfect for outdoor activities.

The city has excellent transportation links, including Phu Cat Airport with domestic and international connections, making it convenient for international participants to attend the conference.

Getting to ICISE

By Air

Phu Cat Airport (UIH) - 35km from ICISE

  • Regular flights from Ho Chi Minh City, Hanoi
  • International connections available
  • Airport shuttle service to ICISE can be arranged
  • Taxi ride approximately 45 minutes

By Train

Dieu Tri Railway Station - 20km from ICISE

  • Trains from major Vietnamese cities
  • Scenic coastal route
  • Local transport to ICISE available
  • Comfortable overnight trains from Ho Chi Minh City

By Car

Highway 1A - National Highway

  • Well-connected by national highways
  • 6 hours drive from Ho Chi Minh City
  • 10 hours drive from Hanoi
  • Car rental services available

By Bus

Quy Nhon Bus Station - 15km from ICISE

  • Regular bus services from major cities
  • Comfortable sleeper buses available
  • Economical travel option
  • Local buses to ICISE

Visa Formalities

Most international visitors to Vietnam require a visa. We recommend checking visa requirements well in advance of your travel dates.

Visa Requirements
  • Valid passport (6+ months remaining)
  • Visa application or approval letter
  • Passport photos
  • Conference invitation letter available
Processing Time
  • E-visa: 3-5 working days
  • Visa on arrival: Pre-approval required
  • Embassy visa: 5-10 working days
  • Apply early to avoid delays
